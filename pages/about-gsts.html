Goals

To attain leadership in Academic degree and Technical Vocational Education and Training (TVET) market in term of student's population, state-of-the art facilities and faculty competencies. By then, GSTS will have contributed a significant share in the local labor market that has national and international certificates. Proportionate measure in resource generation, capacity building and institutional partners shall be guided in that direcion.

Objectives

  • Provide vision and leadership in the evaluation and implementation of advance technologies;
  • Operate multi-level technical learning institution integrated with ethical values to meet global excellence competitiveness; and
  • Ensure better learning environment;
  • Continously upgrade and improve our facilities based on the current technology;
  • Provide usable and easy access to infromation srvices;
  • Develop workers equipped with competitive employable skills for middle level manpower;
  • Serve as training venue for skills development based on the needs of the country
